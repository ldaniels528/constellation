Layout Functions

Attribute Management

  • Color Management - provides a means of managing colors
  • Filter Management - provides a means of managing filters
  • Picture Management - provides a means of managing pictures
  • Set Background - provides a means of setting the canvas' background image

Creation Functions

  • COMMENT - facilitates the creation of an angled line
  • SKETCH - facilitates the creation of a line between two points
  • TEXTNOTE - facilitates the creation of a line between two points

Measurement Functions

  • DIMENSION - facilitates the creation of a horizontal line
  • MEASURE - facilitates the creation of a line that is normal (perpendicular) to a second line

Modification Functions

  • FOLD - facilitates the joining of two lines
  • MIRROR - facilitates the breaking of a line into two new lines
  • ROTATE - facilitates the trimming or extending of a line to a second line or point
  • SCALE - facilitates the joining of two lines
  • TRANSLATE - facilitates the breaking of a line into two new lines
